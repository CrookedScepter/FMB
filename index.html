The Flying Microtonal Banana

A King Gizzard guitar experience

What's up with this banana?

What is the banana?

The banana is a custom-built microtonal guitar. The guitar has extra frets added, allowing "microtones" to be played.

Where did the banana come from?

From King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard's 2017 album of the same name: Flying Microtonal Banana.

Why is it called the Flying Microtonal Banana?

It plays microtones and it looks like a banana, what else do you want?

Where can I hear the banana?

King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard have put out three Microtonal albums. You can hear the Banana being played on the following tracks:
  • Rattlesnake
  • Billabong Valley
  • Nuclear Fusion
  • Flying Microtonal Banana
  • K.G.L.W. (Intro)
  • Minimum Brain Size
  • Intrasport
  • If Not Now, Then When?
  • Pleura
  • K.G.L.W. (Outro)
